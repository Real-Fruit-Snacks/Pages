NAME
       buildah-rename - Rename a local container.

SYNOPSIS
       buildah rename container new-name

DESCRIPTION
       Rename a local container.

EXAMPLE
       buildah rename containerName NewName

       buildah rename containerID NewName

       buildah(1)
