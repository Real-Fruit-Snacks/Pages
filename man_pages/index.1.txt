GRASS GIS 7.8.4 Reference Manual

Geographic
Resources Analysis Support System, commonly referred to
as GRASS GIS, is a Geographic Information System (GIS) used
for geospatial data management and analysis, image
processing, graphics/maps production, spatial modeling, and
visualization. GRASS is currently used in academic and
commercial settings around the world, as well as by many
governmental agencies and environmental consulting
companies.

This reference
manual details the use of modules distributed with
Geographic Resources Analysis Support System (GRASS), an
open source (GNU GPLed), image processing and geographic
information system (GIS).
