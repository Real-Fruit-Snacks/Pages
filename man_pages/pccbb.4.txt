TUNABLES

The driver supports the
following tunable parameters, which may be added to
/boot/loader.conf or set via the sysctl(8)
command:

hw.cbb.debug

Non-zero values
cause more verbose information to be printed to aid in
debugging problems with the bridge chipset.
