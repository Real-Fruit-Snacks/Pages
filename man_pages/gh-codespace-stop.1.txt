NAME
       gh-codespace-stop - Stop a running codespace

SYNOPSIS
       gh codespace stop [flags]

OPTIONS
       -c, --codespace <string>
              Name of the codespace

       -o, --org <login>
              The login handle of the organization (admin-only)

       -u, --user <username>
              The username to stop codespace for (used with --org)
