NAME
       gh-gist-list - List your gists

SYNOPSIS
       gh gist list [flags]

OPTIONS
       -L, --limit <int>
              Maximum number of gists to fetch

       --public
              Show only public gists

       --secret
              Show only secret gists

SEE ALSO
       gh-gist(1)
