SYNOPSIS

xfv
[options] parameter-file

DESCRIPTION

Vstream is a
collection of utilities for capturing reasonably high
quality video from a v4l device (specifically configured for
bttv). A simultaneous audio track is also captured.

This utility -
xfv - is a viewer for frame-by frame viewer of edited
streams under X windows (for testing/aligning edits).

OPTIONS

-double

display images twice normal
size.

parameter-file

the parameter file.
