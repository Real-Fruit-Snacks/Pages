NAME

zzz &mdash; suspend an
ACPI or APM system

SYNOPSIS

zzz

DESCRIPTION

The zzz utility checks
for ACPI or APM support and then suspends the system
appropriately. For APM,

apm -z

will be issued.
For ACPI, the configured suspend state will be looked up,
checked to see if it is supported and,

acpiconf -s

will be
issued.

SEE ALSO

acpi(4), apm(4), acpiconf(8),
apm(8)
