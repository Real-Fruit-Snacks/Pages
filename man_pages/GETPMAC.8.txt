GETPMAC(8)

NAME

getpmac &mdash; print
process-related MAC labels

SYNOPSIS

getpmac
[&minus;l list,of,labels]
[&minus;p pid]

DESCRIPTION

The getpmac utility
prints the text representation of the MAC label associated
with the specified process. If no process is specified, it
prints the label associated with its own process.
