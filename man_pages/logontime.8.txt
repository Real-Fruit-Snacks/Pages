Name

logontime
&mdash; show cumulative logon time from wtmp

Syntax

logontime
[-t]

Description

Examines the
output of &rsquo;last -ax&rsquo; (depends on /var/log/wtmp)
and calculates the cumulative logon time for each user.

Options

-t

Sort by logon time rather than
alphabetically.

hxtools(7)
