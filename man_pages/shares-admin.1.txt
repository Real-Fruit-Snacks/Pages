NAME

shares-admin -
Shared Folders Administration Tool

SYNOPSIS

shares-admin
[OPTION...]

DESCRIPTION

shares-admin
is part of the GNOME system tools, a set of tools to easily
access and manage system configuration.

shares-admin
allows you to share folders to other computers in your
network or in the internet.

OPTIONS

shares-admin
accepts the standard GNOME and GTK options.
