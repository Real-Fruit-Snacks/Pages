NAME

kgdb &mdash; The kernel
debugger

SYNOPSIS

kgdb [-v]
[-d crashdir]
[-c core | -n dumpnr | -r device]
[kernel [core]]

DESCRIPTION

The kgdb utility is a
debugger based on gdb(1) that allows debugging of kernel
core files.

SEE ALSO

gdb(1)

HISTORY

The kgdb utility first
appeared in its current form in FreeBSD 5.3.
