UNVIS(1) BSD General Commands Manual UNVIS(1)

NAME

unvis &mdash; revert a
visual representation of data back to original form

SYNOPSIS

unvis
[file ...]

DESCRIPTION

unvis is the inverse
function of vis(1). It reverts a visual representation of
data back to its original form on standard output.

SEE ALSO

vis(1), unvis(3), vis(3)

HISTORY

The unvis command appears
in 4.4BSD.
