NAME

pbmlife - apply
Conway&rsquo;s rules of Life to a PBM image

SYNOPSIS

pbmlife
[pbmfile]

DESCRIPTION

This program is
part of Netpbm(1).

pbmlife
reads a PBM image as input, applies the rules of Life to it
for one generation, and produces a PBM image as output.

A white pixel
in the image is interpreted as a live beastie, and a black
pixel as an empty space.

OPTIONS

There are no
command line options defined specifically for
pbmlife, but it recognizes the options common to all
programs based on libnetpbm (See
 Common Options
.)
