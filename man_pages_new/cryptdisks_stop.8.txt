NAME

cryptdisks_stop
- wrapper around cryptsetup that parses /etc/crypttab.

SYNOPSIS

cryptdisks_stop

DESCRIPTION

cryptdisks_stop
is a wrapper around cryptsetup that parses
/etc/crypttab just like the initscript
/etc/init.d/cryptdisks does and stops the dm-crypt mapping
that corresponds to .
