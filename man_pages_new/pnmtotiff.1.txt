NAME

replaced by pamtotiff

DESCRIPTION

This program is
part of Netpbm(1).

In Netpbm 10.30
(October 2005), pnmtotiff was extended and renamed to
pamtotiff(1).

pamtotiff
is backward compatible with pnmtotiff.
