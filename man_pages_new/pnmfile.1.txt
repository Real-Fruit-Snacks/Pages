NAME

pnmfile -
replaced by pamfile

DESCRIPTION

This program is
part of Netpbm(1).

pnmfile
was replaced in Netpbm 10.9 (September 2002) by
pamfile(1).

pamfile
is backward compatible with pnmfile, but works on PAM
images too.
