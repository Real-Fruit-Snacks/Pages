NAME

hpfsck &mdash;
check integrity of an HFS+ volume

SYNOPSIS

hpfsck
[-v] source-path

Description

hpfsck
checks the integrity of an HFS+ volume. With the -v
option, the data contained in the volume header is output as
well.

See also

hfsplus(7),
hpmount(1), hpls(1), hpcd(1), hprm(1), hpmkdir(1), hppwd(1),
hpcopy(1), hpumount(1).
