NAME

time &minus;
Prints the given time in UTC and Local Time in human
readable form.

SYNOPSIS

time
time

OPTIONS

time

is the number of seconds since
epoch. 

DESCRIPTION

time is
a helper program from the package nvram-wakeup.

EXAMPLES

# time
1089365289 
(time_t) 1089365289 
(local time) Fri Jul 9 11:28:09 2004 
(utc/gmt) Fri Jul 9 09:28:09 2004
