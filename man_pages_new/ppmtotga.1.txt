NAME

ppmtotga -
replaced by pamtotga

DESCRIPTION

This program is
part of Netpbm(1).

ppmtotga
was replaced in Netpbm 10.6 (July 2002) by
pamtotga(1).

pamtotga
is backward compatible with ppmtotga, but also
recognizes PAM input, including that with a transparency
channel.
