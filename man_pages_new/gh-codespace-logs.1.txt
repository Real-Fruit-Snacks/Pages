NAME

gh-codespace-logs
- Access codespace logs

SYNOPSIS

gh codespace
logs [flags]

OPTIONS

-c,
--codespace 

Name of the codespace

-f, --follow

Tail and follow the logs

-R, --repo

Filter codespace selection by
repository name (user/repo)

--repo-owner

Filter codespace selection by
repository owner (username or org)

SEE ALSO

gh-codespace(1)
