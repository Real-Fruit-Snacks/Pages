NAME

gh-codespace-ports-forward
- Forward ports

SYNOPSIS

gh codespace
ports forward :...
[flags]

OPTIONS INHERITED FROM PARENT COMMANDS

-c,
--codespace 

Name of the codespace

-R, --repo

Filter codespace selection by
repository name (user/repo)

--repo-owner

Filter codespace selection by
repository owner (username or org)
